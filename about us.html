About us

Our application was created in March 2021.
It is a web application that manages the information of banking transactions within a private company. The company keeps a financial record every month through a list of bank transfers and information about it.
The application allows you to manage the information of banking transactions within a private company. The computer system includes a user interface, a database server that will store information about banking transactions, application server.
Depending on the type of authentication (admin or user), the user will be allowed to manage the information or only view, upload or download it. The system will offer the possibility to upload or download banking transactions and manage them.
